A tiny bit about me

I am Sous-Chef with 10 years of experience working in some of the most exciting kitchens in NYC. In my most recent role, I was tasked with overseeing the daily workflow of the restaurant, while maintaining communication between the kitchen, PR, and hotel departments. Something that I also enjoy is being able to nurture the next generations of cooks that walk into the kitchen. In early 2020, my fiance and I had big plans to start a family, mainly having kids. But as a Chef, It's really hard to find a healthy balance between work and family. I want to be a part of my kids growing up. And to do that I needed a Career Change.
I was looking for a career where I can be proud to be a part of, where the lessons learned in the kitchen can give me a leg up in the process, a career where the quality of work I produce speaks louder than any words in the dictionary. Interestingly I found it in coding. I’ve learned a ton in Springboard, from frontend and backend development to databases, data structures, and algorithms. I’m by no means an expert, but after dipping my toes in coding. I’m hungry for more.
I am pursuing a software engineer position in which I can contiune to progress as a developer, to contribute, be part of a great team and share my knowledge with others.
